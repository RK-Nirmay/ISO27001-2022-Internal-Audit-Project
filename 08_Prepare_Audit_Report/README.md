# Stage 08 - Prepare and Distribute the Audit Report

The audit report is the formal output of the audit. It brings together the scope, objectives,
methodology, and findings into a single document for top management and stakeholders, and sets out
the recommendations and next steps. It is distributed promptly after the audit.

## Contents

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `Final_Audit_Report.docx` | The complete internal audit report. |

## What the report covers

- Executive summary.
- Scope and objectives of the audit.
- Audit criteria and methodology.
- Audit findings: overall conformity, the three Minor nonconformities, the two opportunities for
  improvement, and a statement that no Major nonconformities were found.
- Traceability of each finding to its criterion, evidence, owner, and status.
- Recommendations and next steps.
- Certification readiness conclusion.
- Distribution list (top management and stakeholders).

## Key facts

- Audit conducted: 16 to 20 March 2026.
- Report issued: 30 March 2026 (within the 14-day reporting window).
- Result: largely conforming and broadly ready for certification, subject to closing three Minor
  nonconformities.

## How it connects

The report consolidates the findings from Stage 06 and the conclusions confirmed at the closing
meeting (Stage 07). The corrective actions it recommends are tracked to closure in Stage 09.
