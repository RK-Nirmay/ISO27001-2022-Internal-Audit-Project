# Stage 05 - Perform Audit Activities

This is the fieldwork stage. Working through the checklists from Stage 03, the auditor gathers
evidence by reviewing documents, interviewing auditees, observing processes, and sampling records.
The activities and evidence are recorded here so that every finding can be traced back to what was
seen and who provided it.

To keep the record concise, all fieldwork is captured in a single workbook rather than several
separate files.

## Contents

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `Audit_Fieldwork_Log.xlsx` | Single workbook with three tabs: Evidence Register (what was examined, by whom, and the result), Interview and Observation Notes (summary of discussions and observations), and Sampling Record (what was sampled, the basis, and the result). |

## What the fieldwork covered

- Document review across Clauses 4 to 10 and the applicable Annex A controls.
- Interviews with all five auditees.
- Observation of secure development, AWS access configuration, encryption, and logging.
- Sampling of user access, code changes, training records, backups, and risk-register entries.

## Findings emerging from fieldwork

The fieldwork confirmed conformity across most of the ISMS, and surfaced the following:

- Minor NC: legal and regulatory requirements not formally identified (Clause 4.2 / A.5.31).
- Minor NC: segregation of duties not formally addressed in the small team (A.5.3).
- Minor NC: periodic access reviews not evidenced (A.5.18 / A.8.2).
- OFI: single-cloud (AWS) dependency with no documented exit plan (A.5.30).
- OFI: training-completion tracking is informal (A.6.3).

These are formalised in Stage 06 (Identify Findings) and carried into the report and corrective
actions.
