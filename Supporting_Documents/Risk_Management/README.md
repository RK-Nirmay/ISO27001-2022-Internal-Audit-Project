# Risk Management

The risk assessment and risk treatment for Stark Industries Inc. are maintained in the combined
workbook, not as separate files in this folder.

See: `Supporting_Documents/Statement_of_Applicability/Stark_Industries_Assets_Risk_Assessment_SoA.xlsx`

- The **Risk Assessment** tab holds the identified risks, scoring, treatment, residual risk, owners,
  and status.
- The methodology is described in `ISMS_Clause_Documents/Risk_Assessment_Process.docx` and
  `ISMS_Clause_Documents/Risk_Treatment_Process_and_Plan.docx`.

A single combined workbook is used so there is one source of truth for assets, risks, and the SoA.
