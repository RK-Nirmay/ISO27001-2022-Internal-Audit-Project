# Stage 04 - Conduct Opening Meeting

The opening meeting formally starts the audit. The auditor and auditees meet before fieldwork to
confirm the scope, objectives, criteria, schedule, and methods, agree points of contact, and give
everyone a chance to raise questions. It sets a shared understanding so the audit runs smoothly.

Activities), where the fieldwork is carried out.
