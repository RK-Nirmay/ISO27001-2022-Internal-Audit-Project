# Stage 07 - Conduct Closing Meeting

The closing meeting formally ends the audit fieldwork. The auditor presents the findings, the
overall conclusion, and the next steps to the auditees, gives them the chance to respond, and
agrees how corrective actions will be handled. It ensures there are no surprises in the written
report.

To keep the record concise, the agenda, minutes, and attendance are held in a single workbook,
matching the format used for the opening meeting.

## Contents
